 🚗 OLA Rides Analysis Dashboard

## 📘 Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of **Ola ride data**, offering insights into ride volume trends, booking status distribution, revenue performance, vehicle preferences, cancellation reasons, and customer/driver ratings.  

The project helps stakeholders understand **rider behavior**, **revenue drivers**, and **operational efficiency** through data-driven visuals and interactive insights.

---

## ✨ Features
- 📅 **Ride Trends Analysis:** Track ride volume and booking activity over time.  
- 📊 **Booking Status Overview:** Compare successful, cancelled, and pending bookings.  
- 🚗 **Vehicle Type Insights:** Identify top vehicle types by ride distance and usage frequency.  
- 💰 **Revenue Dashboard:** Analyze revenue by payment method, customer segment, and ride distance.  
- ❌ **Cancellation Analytics:** Understand cancellation patterns and reasons from both customers and drivers.  
- ⭐ **Ratings Insights:** Explore distribution of customer and driver ratings to assess satisfaction and performance.  
- 🧭 **Interactive Filters & Drill-Throughs:** Filter by city, date, vehicle type, or payment method for deeper analysis.  
- 📈 **Dynamic KPIs:** Automatically updated metrics such as total rides, total revenue, and cancellation rates.
